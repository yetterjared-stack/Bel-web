01 — About

Building Partnerships That Endure

Established in 1997, Belgravia Management is a Philadelphia-based single-family office founded by Ira Lubert.

Belgravia makes opportunistic investments across various industries and asset classes, with a strong focus on direct investments in real estate, operating businesses, debt, and venture opportunities. The firm aspires to make meaningful, yet minority, investments with long-term partners.

Ira Lubert

Founder

Today, Mr. Lubert's primary focus is Belgravia Management and its family office activities. Over his career, Mr. Lubert had over 50 years of experience in real estate and private equity. He co-founded Lubert-Adler Partners, a real estate private equity firm, and co-founded seven other private fund managers, including Independence Capital Partners, where he served as Co-Founder and Chairman.

Throughout his career, Mr. Lubert was actively involved in both private and public corporate boards and raised funds for numerous charitable causes. His board service included the National Constitution Center, Penn State University (where he retired as Chair), and Jefferson Hospital, among others.

02 — Investment Focus

Flexible Capital Across Markets

Belgravia engages in a broad range of investment activities across the capital spectrum. Primary areas of investment include real estate, direct equity and debt investments in private companies, and alternative investment vehicles such as private equity, venture capital, and hedge funds.

01

Real Estate

Belgravia is an active real estate investor, engaging in a wide range of transactions across the capital structure. The firm invests in self-managed and developer-backed projects, including ground-up development, vertical construction, stabilized properties, and other real estate opportunities.

The firm has a strong preference for locations in and around the Northeast and Southeast United States, but maintains flexibility in its deployment strategy to capitalize on compelling opportunities.

02

Operating Businesses

Belgravia specializes in providing flexible capital to support operating businesses across a wide range of industries, company stages, and security types. The focus is on finding committed, ethical, and capable partners who prioritize conduct, reputation, and legacy.

The firm strives to make meaningful, but minority, investments with these partners, providing both capital and strategic guidance to support long-term success.

03

Venture Capital

With over 50 years of experience investing in venture companies, Belgravia leverages its extensive expertise and relationships to support the growth and success of its portfolio companies.

The firm is most active in early to mid-stage companies, backing committed entrepreneurs with bold, disruptive ideas that have the potential to create meaningful market impact.

04

Alternative Investments

Belgravia maintains a diverse portfolio of co-investments and alternative investment vehicles, including venture capital funds, private equity funds, and hedge funds.

This approach capitalizes on a wide range of opportunities, leveraging the expertise of specialized fund managers to achieve superior risk-adjusted returns.

05

Public Equities

Belgravia maintains an active public equities portfolio, pursuing both opportunistic investments and a concentrated portfolio of blue-chip stocks with long-term appreciation potential.

The firm seeks compelling risk-adjusted returns across market conditions, leveraging deep fundamental analysis and a disciplined investment framework.

06

Debt Investments

Belgravia is an active lender across asset classes, deploying both traditional and flexible capital solutions. The firm provides senior secured loans and other traditional financing structures, as well as creative capital solutions for developers, operating businesses, and private equity funds.

This cross-asset approach enables Belgravia to capture attractive risk-adjusted yields while supporting partners who require bespoke financing structures.
