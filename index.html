I

Real Estate

Active across the capital structure — from vertical construction to stabilized assets.

Belgravia invests across a broad range of real estate transactions, including self-managed and developer-backed projects spanning vertical construction, stabilized properties, and opportunistic acquisitions. We maintain a preference for the Northeast and Southeast United States, while remaining flexible to pursue compelling opportunities wherever they arise.

II

Operating Businesses

Meaningful, minority capital for operators who prioritize conduct, reputation, and legacy.

Belgravia provides flexible capital to operating businesses across industries and stages. Our structural preference is minority — we support operators, not replace them — and we back committed, ethical partners with both capital and strategic guidance.

III

Venture Capital

Early and mid-stage conviction, drawn from five decades of operator relationships.

With over 50 years of venture investing experience, Belgravia leverages deep expertise and long-standing relationships to support portfolio companies through growth. We are most active at the early to mid-stage, backing entrepreneurs with bold ideas and the discipline to execute.

IV

Alternative Investments

A diverse book of co-investments and fund commitments alongside specialized managers.

Belgravia maintains a diversified portfolio of co-investments and alternative vehicles — spanning venture capital, private equity, and hedge funds. This approach harnesses the expertise of specialized managers to broaden our opportunity set and pursue superior risk-adjusted returns.

V

Public Equities

Concentrated positions in blue-chip franchises, held with patience across cycles.

Belgravia maintains an active public equities portfolio — combining opportunistic investments with a concentrated book of blue-chip positions held for long-term appreciation. Our approach is grounded in deep fundamental analysis and a disciplined framework for evaluating risk-adjusted returns across market conditions.

VI

Debt Investments

Traditional and bespoke financing structures underwritten across the capital stack.

Belgravia deploys both traditional and flexible capital solutions across asset classes. We provide senior secured loans alongside creative financing for developers, operating companies, and private equity funds — capturing attractive yields while serving partners who require bespoke structures.
