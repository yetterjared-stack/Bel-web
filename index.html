01 — About

Building Partnerships That Endure

Established in 1997, Belgravia Management is a Philadelphia-based single-family office founded by Ira Lubert.

The firm makes opportunistic investments across various industries and asset classes, with a strong focus on direct investments in real estate, operating businesses, debt, and venture opportunities. We aspire to make meaningful, yet minority, investments with long-term partners.

Ira Lubert

Founder

Mr. Lubert has over 40 years of experience in real estate and private equity. He is a Co-Founder of Lubert-Adler Partners, a real estate private equity firm, and has co-founded seven other private fund managers, including Independence Capital Partners, where he serves as Co-Founder and Chairman.

Throughout his career, Mr. Lubert has been actively involved in both private and public corporate boards and has raised funds for numerous charitable causes. His board service includes the National Constitution Center, Penn State University (where he retired as Chair), and Jefferson Hospital, among others.

02 — Investment Focus

Flexible Capital Across Markets

Belgravia engages in a broad range of investment activities across the capital spectrum. Our primary areas of investment include real estate, direct equity and debt investments in private companies, and alternative investment vehicles such as private equity, venture capital, and hedge funds.

01

Real Estate

Belgravia is an active real estate investor, engaging in a wide range of transactions across the capital structure. We invest in self-managed and developer-backed projects, including ground-up development, vertical construction, stabilized properties, and other real estate opportunities.

We have a strong preference for locations in and around the Northeast and Southeast United States, but remain flexible with our deployment strategy to capitalize on compelling opportunities.

02

Operating Businesses

We specialize in providing flexible capital to support operating businesses across a wide range of industries, company stages, and security types. Our focus is on finding committed, ethical, and capable partners who prioritize conduct, reputation, and legacy.

We strive to make meaningful, but minority, investments with these partners, providing both capital and strategic guidance to support long-term success.

03

Venture Capital

With over 40 years of experience investing in venture companies, we leverage our extensive expertise and relationships to support the growth and success of our portfolio companies.

We are most active in early to mid-stage companies, backing committed entrepreneurs with bold, disruptive ideas that have the potential to create meaningful market impact.

04

Alternative Investments

Belgravia maintains a diverse portfolio of co-investments and alternative investment vehicles, including venture capital funds, private equity funds, and hedge funds.

Our approach to fund investing allows us to capitalize on a wide range of opportunities, leveraging the expertise of specialized fund managers to achieve superior risk-adjusted returns.
